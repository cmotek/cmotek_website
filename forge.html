My forge is where I account for the projects that I develop on my own. I believe that if I show that programming is both a profession and a passion I will make a better argument to an employer about hiring me as a developer. My forge is also a way for me to show my personality and interests. The forge is a set of HTML cards that contain text and images that tell a story about how I have grown as a creator. It is also a way for me to appreciate my path to becoming a developer and a measuring stick of my progress.
